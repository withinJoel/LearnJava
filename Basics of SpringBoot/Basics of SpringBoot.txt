* Create a new project in IntelliJ IDEA
# In the `New Project` window.
    * Select `Spring Initializr`.
    * Click `Next`.
# Configure the project settings
   - Group: `com.example`
   - Artifact: `demo`
   - Name: `demo`
   - Type: `Gradle Project`
   - Language: `Java`
   - Click `Next`.
# Select dependencies
   * In the `Dependencies` section, add the following:
   - `Spring Web`
   - Click `Next`, then `Finish`.
